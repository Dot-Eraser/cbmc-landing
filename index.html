About CBMC

Who We Are

The California Buddhist Meditation Center (CBMC) is a community-driven nonprofit effort to build a spiritual and cultural home for Bangladeshi Buddhists and our friends in California.

CBMC began from the simple need for a shared space‚Äîwhere families can come together to practice meditation, celebrate Buddhist festivals, and preserve our Bangladeshi Buddhist heritage for the next generation.

We are working toward establishing a center that can host religious programs, meditation sessions, Dhamma talks, children‚Äôs education, and cultural activities all under one roof.

Our community is open and welcoming. Whether you are a long-time resident, a new immigrant, or a friend of the Bangladeshi Buddhist community, you are warmly invited to participate.
